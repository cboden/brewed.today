Beyond The Pale

Ottawa

Focus

The third in the series of beers that is brewed with only one malt and one hop to help discover the wonderful and unique flavours of each ingredient.For this iteration we brewed a 5% ABV ale with Munich malt and Pacifica hops. This beer is a stunning clear copper orange when pouring, boasting a bready aroma with a subtle hint of orange. Crispy AF with a soft malty body and a smooth bitterness, this beer will leave you desperate for more. We suggest drinking in Steins to enjoy the full experience.
