FAQ

Q - Will these beers be available on tap or in the retail shop on their own?

A - Yes! All of these beers will be available for sale as single cans and on tap, it is first come first serve and once they're gone, they're gone.

Q - When can I pick up my gift box?

A - Gift boxes will be available for pick up starting November 28, 2023.

Q - Can I ship a gift box?

A - Gift boxes are available for shipping within Ontario, shipping is calculated at checkout.

Q - Is local delivery available?

A - Yes, we offer free local delivery. If you have difficulty with this selection at checkout, please contact us at celebrate@redtapebrewery.com.

Q - It's a pretty plan box, is gift wrap available?

A - Yes, you can get this gift wrapped for an additional $10.

Q - Why 8?

A - We're a tiny brewery with limited capacity. We chose 8 as we imagined you may want to share these beers with a friend over the holidays. By having 3 of each, you could give one to a friend and if you love it, you still have one more!
