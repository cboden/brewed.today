Bourbon Barrel aged Belgian Quadrupel

We're pening the cellar for a super limited release to coincide with our other Bourbon barrel release last week. Absolute Monarchy: Trap King, a Kentucky Bourbon barrel aged version of our Belgian Quadrupel is now available!

Brewed nearly 5 years ago, this Quad was aged in Kentucky Bourbon barrels then blended and bottle conditioned for 3 more years. This delicious dark ale, fermented with our favourite Belgian Trappist yeast strain, has only gotten more nuanced with time.

Clocking in at 12.6% abv, this big, bold Belgian Quad packs sweet waves of raisin, fig, and plum, with alternating notes of maple syrup, molasses and burnt caramel. The original smokey Bourbon overtones have mellowed to a smooth, yet decadent flavour with a hint of spice on the swallow from the yeast and gentle warming sensation from the alcohol.
