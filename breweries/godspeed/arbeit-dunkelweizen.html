Arbeit Dunkelweizen

$3.75

Arbeit Dunkelweizen is one of two collaboration beers we brewed with our good friend Michael Hancock. Hancock's storied brewing career takes root back in 1989 at Denison's Brewing Company, where he brewed Bavarian-style beers with class and distinction. His wheat beer in particular became world-renowned for its incredible flavour and fidelity to tradition. Michael then went on to help establish Sidelaunch, who continue to brew his prized recipes to this day.

Arbeit means "work" in German and after 40 years of brewing and engineering, no one encapsulates the word more than our friend and collaborator. This dark wheat beer features a soft texture and wonderful structure; embedded by notes of banana, clove, and hazelnut, and distant praline.

(5.0% ABV) - 355mL Can
