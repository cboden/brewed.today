Oi!

Oi ! Britain and Japan might be on opposite sides of the world, but we feel like we have brought them closer together via some informal slang in Toronto. We brewed Oi ! as an ESB (Extra Special Bitter). Soft orange marmalade aroma immerses you and is followed by a biscuity toffee body. There is a slight peppery and grassy finish that rounds off this very well balanced beer. British Pale Ale and Speciality malts with 100% East Kent Golding hops brings a truly authentic British beer style to this part of the world. Oi! & Cheers!
