Saison of the Witch (Pink Boots)

$3.67

Saison of the Witch is a farmhouse saison brewed with the 2022 Pink Boots hop blend from Yakima Chief Hops and finished with a huge dry hop of the same hops and hibiscus, rose hips and flowers.

Starting out with a effervescent carbonation that works to lift up aromas and flavours of berry, currents, light tropical fruit, citrus and melon with herbal and floral accents.

The finish is dry with notes of black pepper from the yeast lingering on the tongue. Cleanly complex complimenting the crisp and refreshing mouthfeel it is a true witches brew.

5.1% ABV | 27 IBU

**Saison of the Witch will be on tap and in cans at Red Barn Brewery while supplies last with a portion of each sale directly going towards the Pink Boots Society Canada chapter.
