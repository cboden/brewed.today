Elevenses

$3.50 + tax

Elevenses, inspired by that essential mid-morning meal sandwiched between Second Breakfast and lunch, is our contribution to Breakfast Club, a series of breakfast-inspired beers organized by Third Moon Brewing out of Milton, ON. Dominion City is one of 16 participating breweries across Ontario who will be releasing breakfast-inspired beers over the coming weeks.

Ours is brewed with real Cholaca (think liquid cocoa) and loaded with bready, chocolatey, maple-y vanilla flavours. Despite the intensity this beer remains highly drinkable and one of our favorite dessert stouts to date!

Each participating Breakfast Club brewery is holding a food drive in their community to celebrate the release of these beers. Everyone deserves to eat.
