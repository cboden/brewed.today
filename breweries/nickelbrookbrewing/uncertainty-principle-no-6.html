Uncertainty Principle No. 6

Another adventurous brew emerges from the Nickel Brook cellar. Our brewers continue to push the boundaries of Barrel-aged sours with a bold Sour Blend aged in Wine Barrels, and matured on Red Currants & Tart Cherries. The result? A deep Mahogany coloured brew, boasting unique complexities that will never be repeated. Inviting aromas of Cherry Balsamic and dried fruits pave the way to a quick tactile tartness of Red Currants, and a graceful shift to a mellowed Oak and Honey finish. A tantalizing and deliciously tannic brew that you will want to keep your eye on.  500mL Bottle | ABV: 7% | IBU: 5 Recommended Glassware: Brandy Glass, Sommelier Glass, Wine Glass
