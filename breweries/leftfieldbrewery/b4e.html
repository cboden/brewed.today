$2 from every Beer For Everybody 6 pack sold supports Foodshare in their work toward access to affordable, fresh and nutritious food for all.

FoodShare has been a leader in the food movement since 1985, they focus on food justice by working to dismantle oppressive structures that hold poverty and food insecurity in place. They collaborate with and take their cue from those most affected by poverty and food insecurity—Black, Indigenous, People of Colour, People with Disabilities. Together they advocate for bold public policy interventions, offer innovative food education and nutrition programs, and increase access to fresh, nutritious food. Each year they reach over 260,000 people. Learn more at foodshare.net
