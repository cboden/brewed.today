This is an amber California-style lager with notes of light toffee and bread crust with undertones of strawberry. A easy drinking and well balanced lager that is full of flavour.

We special ordered a California lager strain that does best when fermented a bit warmer than a typical lager. This adds more fruit flavour than a typical lager train.
