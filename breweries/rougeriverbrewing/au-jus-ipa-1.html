Au Jus IPA #1

Au Jus IPA #1 [NEIPA] 6.8% abv. The first in our new series of rotating IPAs, this is a hazy IPA hopped with NZ Nelson Sauvin and US cryo Citra hops for a blast of pineapple, mango and gooseberry character with a little dankness. Crushable and juicy, and the first IPA to come out of our new tanks. Wheat and oats added for haze and body. Fermented with the Vermont yeast strain.
