Island of Imaginary Friends

$17.00

Style: Sticky Mango DIPA

ABV:   8.2%

Tasting Notes

If our minds can imagine magical talking sock puppets, conjuring a luscious double IPA that tastes like mango sticky rice shouldn’t be too hard.  Inspired by various incarnations of the delicious Southeast Asian dessert, this friendly, imaginative beer shares the happy place of the Flying Monkeys.  Brewed with rice flakes for a buoyant body, ripe mango and fluffy coconut erupt in an explosion of fuzzy, tropical hops settling into an island of fresh sweetness.  
After all, isn't craft brewing basically about taking ideas that exist in your head and making them real?
