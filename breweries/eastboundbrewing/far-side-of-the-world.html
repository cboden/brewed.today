Eastbound Brewing • Toronto

Far Side of the World

$3.75

An irresponsible amount of New Zealand and Australian hops come together in this stunning New England IPA and take you on an adventure to the Far Side of the World. Tropical fruit (think - mango, apricot, orange juice), touch of pine and a full, hazy and thicc creamy body give you your new favourite beer.

Enigma (AUS), Vic Secret (AUS), Rakau (NZ) and Galaxy (AUS) hops all combine in this double dry hopped New England IPA that’s high on hop flavour with almost no bitterness  the trip to the Far Side of the World is a smooth and flavourful one  

ABV: 6.1
Smells & Tastes Like: Mango, apricot & adventure
Food Pairing: Butter poached lobster, ceviche, creamy blue cheese
