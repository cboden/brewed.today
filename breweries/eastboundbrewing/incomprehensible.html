What exactly is a Grisette anyways?

Simply put, it’s the saison version of a session IPA. A Grisette is a lower alcohol and very sessionable version of a Belgian saison.

Incomprehensible Grisette is full of peppery spice, citrus and offers a full compliment of the whole fruit salad bowl. Belgian spice and a fruity dry hop round out this beauty that’s made for a nice session.

 

ABV: 3.2%

Smells & Tastes Like: Pexpert, light & fruit salad
