Slack Tide

Life would be a little easier if we all just gave each other a little slack. A little room to move, a little more understanding and a little bit of calm. Slack Tide - Session New England IPA is here for just that. It's an easy going little beer that's full of flavour and all about finding an empty hammock in the shade.Sabro and Idaho 7 hops combine with New England ale yeast to create a hazy, hazy treat full of flavours of coconut, melon and citrus. The comforting lower ABV of 4.2% lets you live at slack tide for as long as you'd like. We can't hold back time and don't have control over as much as we'd like but Just maybe this will help slow down life just enough for you to catch up.

ABV: 4.2%
Smells & Tastes Like: Coconut, melon & hazy days
Food Pairing: Fish tacos, mango salsa & pulled pork
