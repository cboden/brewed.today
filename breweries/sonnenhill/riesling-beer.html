Riesling Beer

7.4% ABV     ––––––––––––––––     750mL
This wine beer was made by blending oak-aged saison with Riesling from Hinterland Wine Company. The result is a product that blurs the lines between wine and beer. 

Classic Riesling aromas of apple, pear, and pineapple. Light American oak, a prickly carbonation, some salinity and a juicy acidity allow for a quenching finish.

One dollar from every 750ml bottle sold goes to organizations working within our community.
