Sunset Yoga in the Pasture (June - August)

$25.00 + tax

Join us for sunset yoga this summer, led by Kate Cebulski - Sunday Yoga Collective. This event series will take place on Wednesdays, outside, in our pasture located to the back of the biergarten. We have lots of space so feel free to invite friends! 

Tickets include a 1-hour practice in our pasture and a drink of your choosing from our pasture bar. A portion of the profits from ticket sales will be donated to the Willibald Community Fund. 

Our practice will begin promptly at 7:00pm so please arrive early to set up your mat!
