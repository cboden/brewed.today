Warrior Woman

Available again on June 8 We created this beer to honour the life of Kelly Meek.  Kelly was a daughter, a wife, a mother, a business woman, a volunteer, a philanthropist and a warrior.  We are proud to carry on her legacy by donating 5% of all sales of this product to Canadian charities focused on the empowerment of women. An APA made the Canadian Way!  Malt from the East Coast, Hops from the West Coast, Yeast from the Niagara Peninsula and Water from the Nation's Capital.  Our American Pale Ale offers aromas of citrus and subtle spice with a balanced bitter finish.
