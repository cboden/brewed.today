Loud & Clear

Loud and Clear Tart Cherry Stout was 100% brewed by women; from the recipe to the brew day to the art on the can. On February 12th we even invited women from all over Hamilton into the brewery to learn more about the brewing process, and lend a hand! This lightly soured stout has a perfect balance of tart cherries and beautiful roasty notes of dark malts.
