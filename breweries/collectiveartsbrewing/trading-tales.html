Trading Tales

Swapping stories and expanding on collective knowledge is one of our favourite parts of collaborating with other breweries. To honour that oh-so-important aspect of the brewing experience, we teamed up with our pals at Dancing Gnome in Pittsburgh to create a unique take on a classic lager. Trading Tales is dry hopped with Strata and Citra hops, giving it a dankness with notes of citrus, tropical passion fruit, and the crisp clean finish you expect from a lager. ARTIST: Docvek, London, UK. Docvek, aka Keiran Gorick-Sandal, has been living and breathing creative art throughout a solid career spanning over 15 years. A well-versed illustrator, he has successfully built an impressive creative portfolio, while working for globally renowned brands such as the BBC, Penguin Books, The Sunday Times, as well as The British Council and Ninja Tune to name a few. Concurrently working as a multi-disciplined creative lead for a West London corporate giant, he has enjoyed a successful track record in managing branding agencies and running in-house design teams, as well as setting the creative tone and feel for dozens of full-fledged national marketing campaigns.
