Royal City Brewing • Guelph

Beer Diversity

$4.05

Created and launched by Ren Navarro in 2018, Beer. Diversity. strives to educate, enlighten, and make beer approachable to a more diverse audience. Ren is no stranger to public speaking and has appeared frequently at workshops and conferences informing and entertaining people with her love of beer.  Additionally, using her perspective as a queer black woman, she has talked extensively about diversity in craft beer with appearances in various forms of media, such as podcasts, television, radio and newspapers. Navarro is working diligently on the front-lines to initiate change both in and out of the industry.

We've paired up with Ren to create this IPA hopped with sabro, loral, citra and huell melon. It pours a hazy orange with an off-white head. With impressions of lemon, rose petal, fresh cut grass, orange, berry punch, coconut, fresh herbs and white pepper. This beer is a perfect refresher for a hot summer day.

6% ABV | 40 IBU
