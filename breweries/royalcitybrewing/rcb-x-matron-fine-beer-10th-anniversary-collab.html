RCB x Matron Fine Beer 10th Anniversary Collab

$3.95 + tax

To celebrate our first anniversary we took a trip to Kingston to do a collab with Stone City Ales. Our two breweries opened their doors in the same week in 2014, and our first collab celebrated this joint milestone. Flash forward 10 years, and Stone City has changed names and hands, but the founding brewer Justin is now the founder of Matron Fine Beer and is still going strong. This beer is a shout out to that original collab in 2015.

This Saison style beer is light and refreshing. The earthy wheat, spicy Belgian yeast, and grassy hops come together to create a perfect lawnmower beer to enjoy after a day of work outside. Of course, enjoying one in the Beer Garden works too.

Farmhouse Inspired Wheat Ale 4.6% ABV
