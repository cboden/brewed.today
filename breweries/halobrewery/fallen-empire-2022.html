Fallen Empire, 2022

$8.00

Imperial Stout with Chocolate, Vanilla, Cinnamon, Chili Peppers and Lactose.

Making a unique imperial stout has been a long-time goal for us: Meet Fallen Empire, a gargantuan, chewy, intense, mole/mayan-style imperial stout. This confluence of cocoa, vanilla, cinnamon, and four kinds of chili peppers presents as Mayan chocolate in beer form. With a nose of subtle cinnamon, flavours of chocolate and vanilla, and a rich dessert-like mouthfeel, Fallen Empire ends with a fiery flourish of spicy chilis that quickly fades into the depths of history.

Shelf-life: 12+ months, store refrigerated or at cellar temperatures.

ABV: 10.0%
Malts: 2-Row (Rahr), Chit, Flaked Oats, Pale Chocolate, Roasted Barley, Chocolate, Special W, Crystal Medium, Carafa Special III
Hops: Centennial, Amarillo
Yeast: US-05
Adjuncts: Lactose, Dextrose, Maltodextrin, Ceylon Cinnamon (Ground), Dried Chipotle Pepper, Dried Guajillo Pepper, Dried Pasilla Pepper, Cholaca (Unsweetened), Vanilla Bean
