Squamish

$18.00

Squamish is an IPA brewed a little differently than todays standard IPAs. While taking influence from the dominant NEIPAs (hazy/juicy) we have also taken some from the classic Westcoast IPAs.

A grain bill that remembers a time when craft beer was simpler, a small but quintessential bittering charge of hops at the beginning of the boil, a high ester yeast strain, a generous dry hop of classic WC hops while using modern NEIPA processes and a water profile leaning more NEIPA than WC.

We get Pineapple, papaya, evergreen sap and muddled citrus. Aside from Germane, this has become an instant favourite of the staff around here. A present yet restrained bitterness makes this pillowy beer have some punch. Each sip invites you to sip again and again.

Enjoy fresh and cool at 5C-7C.

Snack Pairing: Classic bar nuts

Meal Pairing: Texas BBQ
