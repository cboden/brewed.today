Royalties

$12.00

Brewed in collaboration with our friends and neighbours at Barncat. Royalties is a Golden sour that we added fleur de sel to and aged in Tequila barrels.

This is our oldest liquid we have ever released. Brewed in July 2019 this beer spent 20 months in barrel before being bottle conditioned for an additional 14 months. This extended aged has really allowed for the Brett funk to mature and become the prominent feature of the beer.

These specific Tequila barrels first served the bourbon industry circa 1998-1999 before begin used by the Tequila industry from 2001-1019 to product Reposado (2-12 month of barrel ageing) Tequila. We get a plethora of brett characteristics such as sour gummy candy, powder candies (Rockets/Sweetarts), juicy stoned fruit and wet hay and underlying nutty notes. Coupled with the obvious tequila flavours and salinity we find the moderate acidity, low tannins and a low level of carbonation to really create a unique and delicious tasting experience. If you're a Tequila fan and an orange wine fan look no further!

Enjoy at a temp of 7C - 12C. The warmer it gets the more funk you'll taste.
