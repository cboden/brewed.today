Matron • Bloomfield

NECK OF THE WOODS

$3.50

Collective Arts Brewing X Matron Fine Beer

An IPA brewed between friends who happen to share a real knack for making beautiful beer. Ryan Morrow, brewmaster of Collective Arts and Matron's own Justin da Silva put their hoppy brains together and came up with this delicious IPA. Combining Collective's approach to hops and higher ABV with our obsession with local ingredients we've ended up with a true collaboration. This IPA is brimming with an abundance of local ingredients from Barn Owl Malt, Pleasant Valley Hops, Escarpment Yeast and Hogan's Wild Flower Honey.

2500On the palette you can expect lots of ripe mango, sweet tropical fruit and gentle cedar undertones. We've kept it balanced, widely aromatic and dripping with rich hop flavour. An IPA sure to soothe even the savage beast. 

Numbers: 6.9% / 355ml / 90% Ontario Ingredients

Pairs well with: catching up with old friends.
