This satisfying red ale is named after the famed Royal Hamilton Light Infantry RHLI, or “Rileys”.

Riley Red is not too heavy and light on the aftertaste. Long smooth body, and just enough Rambling cross hops to finish the beer without being overly hoppy. Irish ale yeast gives it a nice biscuit nose and flavour.
