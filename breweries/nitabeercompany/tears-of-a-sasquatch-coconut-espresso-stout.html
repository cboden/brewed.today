Nita Beer Company • Ottawa

Tears of a Sasquatch - Coconut Espresso Stout

$5.00

Style - StoutTasting Notes – Chocolate, Espresso and Toasted CoconutABV – 5.8%Colour – dark brownIBUs – ~lowLegend Rating - Adventure - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
It's a little known fact that the infrequent cries of the sasquatch produce a delicious concoction of coffee and coconut.  Finding some of this liquid gold is on par with the pot of gold at the end of the rainbow, a unicorn in your backyard or a sighting of the elusive sasquatch. Andy cold brewed and pasteurized Equator Coffee espresso and then blended with our  Perfectum stout that was dry-coconutted (like dry-hopping but with coconut).  The result is a beer that Andy would love to drink for breakfast but Bridget says he can only drink after noon (except for what Andy calls Quality Control).
Ingredients – Water, Malted barley, Hops, Yeast, Coffee, Coconut.
