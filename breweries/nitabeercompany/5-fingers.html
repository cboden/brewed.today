5 Fingers

$3.50

Style - Brown Ale
Tasting Notes – toffee, caramel, earthy, malty
ABV – 5.5%
Colour – brown
IBUs – ~40
Legend Rating - Anytime - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 

An American Brown Ale, 5 Fingers offers a full-flavoured, gorgeously coloured beer that is often enjoyed at a leisurely pace. Hop aroma and flavour are less evident than in an IPA, but balance by a stronger bitterness from the hops.

A hint of toffee or caramel on the nose compliments the earthy tones and roasted malty flavour on the pallet. The aftertaste of this beer is strongly influenced by the serving temperature.

With its deep copper colour, 5 Fingers looks as good as it tastes.

Ingredients – Canadian two row, Melanoiden, Carafa II and Special W malts. Nugget, Crystal and Goldings hops.
