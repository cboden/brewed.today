Percy LeFroy Mapleton, aka the British "railway murderer" of 1881. No passenger nor porter witnessed his ghoulish crime on the express train from London Bridge Station. He became the subject of the first police composite picture to appear on a "wanted" poster ultimately leading to his arrest and execution. Our Porter is spookily delicious with hints of cocoa and notes of biscuit, a deep brown hue with full rounded flavour and subtle floral aroma from the Golding hop additions.
