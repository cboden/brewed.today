Ready to get into sours?  Pineapple Sour is a delicious sour for craft connoisseurs and people trying to see if they like sour beers. A Berliner Weisse sour beer fermented with pineapple puree. It is a sweet and sour beer with hints of citrus aroma and refreshing pineapple flavour.
