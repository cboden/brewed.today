#magical!  Our Brut IPA is a unique IPA style with super tropical aromas and flavour.  Think of Brut champagne but for beer (0° Plato means all of the sugars are fermented), with super tropical aromas and flavour, unfiltered yet bright. Could such a mystically delicious concoction be real? Yep. Easy drinking with a dry crisp finish. The perfect beverage to enjoy during the summer or while dreaming of the impossible.
