Pink Boots Session IPA – Taproom Series
Style: Session IPA
ABV: 4% IBU: 40

Brewed as part of our Taproom Series, Pink Boots Session IPA is a bright, easy-drinking IPA with real flavour impact and purpose behind every pour. Light in alcohol but full in character, this crisp and refreshing IPA is lively with citrus aromatics up front. Think grapefruit peel, lemon zest, and subtle tropical notes that are balanced by gentle bitterness and a clean, dry finish.

Pink Boots Collaboration
Brewed in collaboration with Pink Boots Society, an international organization supporting women and non-binary individuals in the fermented beverage industry through education, mentorship, and community. A portion of proceeds from the sale of this beer goes directly to Pink Boots Society, giving accounts and consumers a meaningful reason to support the pour.
