Beatin' The Odds w/ Belma & Ella

$3.70 + tax

Get ready to welcome back "Beatin the Odds," our New England IPA with a brand new recipe and fresh off of its winter hibernation.

This hazy and golden brew pours with a thick and creamy head, releasing an intense burst of tropical fruit aromas such as pineapple and mango, with hints of citrus and a subtle spiciness.

On the palate, "Beatin the Odds" showcases the flavors of Australian Ella and Bellma hops in perfect harmony, with notes of passionfruit, melon, and grapefruit, followed by a mild bitterness that balances out the sweetness. The mouthfeel is soft and velvety, with a medium body that finishes with a refreshing dryness, leaving you wanting more. Cheers!

Alcohol: 5.4%
IBU: 50 out of 100
Malts: 4
Hops: 3
