Sleeping Giant Brewing

Ryebernate IPA

$2.92

The Ryebernate IPA is an amber coloured IPA made with a certain percentage of rye malt, in this case 20% of the malt bill is rye, which will add a distinctive spiciness to the beer. Coming in at 60 IBUs, the perceived bitterness is balanced out with the spice and an increased alcohol content. Hops include Warrior for bittering, then Cascade and Chinook later in the boil for an added pine and citrus character. Coupled with a complex malt bill and low carbonation, the result is a nice winter warmer.
