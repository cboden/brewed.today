Hive

This golden and hazy double IPA leads with delicate aromas of honey and wildflowers, with a hint of tropical fruit juice and freshly cut grass. Clementine and other sweet citrus flavours are enhanced by the smooth mouthfeel and balanced by a lightly bitter finish, With subtle notes of honey throughout from the addition of 13kg to the boil, Hive features the colour, aromas, and flavours of a sunny summer day.
