YOYO (Raspberry, Blueberry & Red Currant)

Sacrilegious? Sure.

Delicious? That too!

YoYo is a Smoothie Sour Beer that's made with loads of smashed raspberries, blueberries and red currant. The addition of marshmallow cream and vanilla take this beer into the dessert-beer territory, but the brightness and slight tartness of the fruit keeps it tasting impossibly fresh. A velvety delight for the senses!

Style: Smoothie Sour
ABV: 5%
Format: 355ml cans

Unfortunately, YoYo is not available for shipping and delivery, but it is available for purchase in our brewpub and bottleshop.
