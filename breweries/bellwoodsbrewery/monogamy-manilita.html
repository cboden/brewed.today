Monogamy Manilita

$5.09 + tax

When Freestyle Hops recently reached out and said they had a small amount of their new experimental hop 'Manilita' available for us, we snatched up as much as we could. 

This was luckily enough to do an entire batch of Monogamy so we could let this baby SHINE.

The same ol' reliable base beer of barley and oats had DOZENS of kilos of Manilita tossed into it which made it ERUPT into flavours and aromas of pineapple ice cream, guava juice and ruby red grapefruit.

We aren't likely to get our hands on this hop for some time now, so please do grab a can, while you still can.
