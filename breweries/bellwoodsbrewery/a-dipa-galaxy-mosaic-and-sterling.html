A DIPA (Galaxy, Mosaic & Sterling)

$5.31

An IPA is a series that acts as a creative outlet for all things DIPA from Oss. A DIPA takes it up a notch. 

In this batch we explore a slightly less dry, rounder hoppy beer. We chose a new (to us) yeast that's less attenuative, modified our grain and water bills, and dosed it with the same massive dry hop that all our DIPAs receive. The resulting beer finishes a little higher, with super aromatics and a soft mouthfeel - in short, it smells and tastes very yummy. 

This batch of A DIPA is hopped with Galaxy, Mosaic and Sterling to create floral aromatics with notes of white freezies and lemon. 

8.8% Alc/Vol | 473 mL can
