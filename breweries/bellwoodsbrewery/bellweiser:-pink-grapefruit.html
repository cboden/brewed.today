Bellweiser: Pink Grapefruit

$5.00

Some people fear that adding citrus to lagers is sacrilegious -- but we are not those people. OG Bellweiser is a flavourful and balanced pilsner that needs no sidekick, but life is short, and pink grapefruits are delicious, so we went ahead and married the two for you. The beer is dry and fruity, it highlights the natural bitterness from the grapefruit, and it easily earns an A+ from us.
