Bellwoods • Toronto

Fruit Helmet 2026 - Golden Raspberry, Peach, Mango

$6.64 + tax

We present to you the 2026 edition of Fruit Helmet — a Bellwoods beer that has taken many forms over our 14 years, but has always remained a collaboration with our friend Jeppe at Evil Twin Brewing in NYC, featuring whatever fruit we’re most excited about at the time.

This year's batch was inspired by a recent batch of Jelly King that featured a solo addition of golden raspberry. We discussed how nice that fruit would be in an IPA accompanied by other thick, nectar-like fruit purees in order to preserve a more viscous body. We landed on peach and mango and requested samples of both from various suppliers in order to properly pick the most primo purees possible. AND BEHOLD!
 
This selection of light-coloured fruits keeps this beer bright and yellow and delicious for the eyes, while the nose and mouth part of your face will be transported to a tropical jungle while sippin' on this juicy, thick, refreshing brew. 
 
It's always nice when the final product matches up with the initial idea of a beer, and this is a prime example of that. It may be another 9 year stretch until we brew this beer again, with cyber fruit and AI juice, so come get some cans ASAP while it still has IRL fruit!
