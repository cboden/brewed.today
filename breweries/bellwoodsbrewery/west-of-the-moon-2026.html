West of the Moon (2026)

$5.31 + tax

One for the East, one for the West!
A few years ago, we brewed West of the Moon while Dominion City made East of the Sun, our very first collabs together. These beers reflected not just where we’re located (Toronto = West, Ottawa = East), but also two distinct styles: an East vs. West Coast IPA.
History repeats itself and we’re back in orbit once again, brewing West of the Moon for this year’s return. 
Our version pours crystal clear with bold notes of tangerine, apricot, and cedar, a proper West Coast crusher if you will.
Together they showcase the multitude of hoppy outcomes that come from similar beginnings, and the abundance inherent in T-E-A-M-W-O-R-K.
