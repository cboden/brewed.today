Bellwoods Brewery

Paper Tiger (Wet-Hopped)

$5.00

Paper Tiger is the Bellwoods interpretation of a ‘new world’ lager, where we aim to combine a traditional pale malt bill and firm bitterness, with the levity of a bright and generous dry hop. Except that in this special edition, we opted for a wet hop rather than dry, utilizing the fleeting, fresh hop flowers that are briefly in season. The result is a slightly more herbal, nuanced effect, with the same outstanding drinkability.
