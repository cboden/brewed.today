Quayle's • Oro-Medonte

The Last Spike

$3.25 + tax

Introducing “Last Spike”, to mark the Year of the Snake and Lunar New Year. A beer that pays tribute to the Chinese workers who helped build Canada’s railway system. The railway has long been a vital connection between east and west, transporting goods from shipping ports to warehouses across the country.

This unique brew starts with our award-winning Line 12 Lager with Szechuan peppercorns and a touch of dried orange peel added. The peppercorns add a subtle berry-like hint rather than heat, while the orange lends a light, candied citrus note. Perfect on its own or paired with your favourite Asian dish. Gung Hey Fat Choy! 🧧🍻
