Quayle's • Oro-Medonte

Little Ripper

$2.95 + tax

The expression “Little Ripper” (pronounced “Lit-l Rip-ah”) is defined as something that is excellent, or goes your way. Like, “It’s my shout mate, so I grabbed you a Quayle’s” “You Little Ripper!”

A unique style in itself, Aussie Pale Ales are lighter in colour and alcohol than their Northern Hemisphere counterparts, and extremely refreshing. Galaxy Hops dominate with heaps of tropical and stone fruit character. Enigma and Eclipse offer additional flavours of grape and citrus. All three of these hops are of Australian origin making it as authentic as you can get (plus our brewer is an Aussie as well!)

Grab some tinnies today and celebrate that summer will soon be here!
