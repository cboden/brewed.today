After almost 5 years, we've hit a milestone. - Batch 1000.We knew we had to do something big and it had to be special, so after months of deliberation amongst our brew team, we decided to go with something few, including ourselves, have ever attempted... the Imperial Granite Bock.

Crafted with only the finest malts, and boiled for several hours to caramelize sugars and add a rich body. We then cooled the young beer and added red hot granite stones that were heated over an open flame to further crystallize the sugars and add a touch of minerality. It was further cellared cold for 3 months to enhance the smooth drinkabilty, and robust, malty finish. Coming in at 10.00% abv, this beer not only ages well, but drinks like a fine whiskey.

This project was definitely a labour of love and undoubtedly one of the most challenging beers we have ever created, but we're are incredibly proud of this one and can't wait for you to give it a try.

Only 1000 bottles made, and will only be made once.Available now at the brewery and online while supplies last.
