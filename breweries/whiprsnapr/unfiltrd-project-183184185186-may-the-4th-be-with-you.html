May the 4th be with you!
Our special May the 4th series release with 4 BRAND NEW beers, and 4 very unique labels!
May the 4th series.  Unfiltrd Project #183 Ben vs Anakin
Cold IPA.   4.8% 40 IBU
Hops: Cascade ekuanot azacca
Cold IPA’s are brewed with more adjuncts than usual, then cold fermented for a lighter, crisper IPA.  (similar to a west coast pilsner)
May the 4th series.  Unfiltrd Project #184 Jar Jar
Milkshake IPA.   5.2% 15 IBU
Hops: Sabro
Big tropical fruit, coconut flavours here from Sabro hops, and the lactose just adds a touch of sweetness to make it pop like a daiquiri!
May the 4th series.  Unfiltrd Project #185 Queen Amidala 
Mountain IPA.   6.2% 45 IBU
Hops: Chinook Summit Citra
Mountain IPA’s are a blend of east and west, meeting somewhere in the middle.  Both east and west coast hops, some haze, some bittnerness.  A fun mix!.
May the 4th series.  Unfiltrd Project #186 Darth Maul
Black DIPA   8% 65 IBU
Hops: CTZ Idaho 7 
Big, black and hard hitting.  Malt heavy roastiness, with a hit of dankness from the Idaho 7 hops.
