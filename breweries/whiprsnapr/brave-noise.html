Unfiltrd Project #154 Brave Noise Pale Ale

5% alc./vol IBU 25

 

Pale ale in the NEIPA style using sabro and mosaic hops.  Notes of guava, kiwi and orange peel – tastes like tropical bubblegum!
What started out as a simple question on Brienne Allan’s Instagram story: 
"Have you ever experienced sexism in the beer industry?" 
quickly became the inquiry that sent shockwaves throughout craft beer, subsequently inspiring a BEVOLUTION. To keep the momentum going; to honor those who have spoken out about their gender discrimination, racism, sexual assault, and harassment; and to bring further awareness to the issues that women, BIPOC, and LGBTQIA+ individuals face in craft beer, we invite you to join us in a collaborative brew titled “Brave Noise.”
Proceeds of the sale of these cans will go to Harmony House. Harmony House is a program, here in Ottawa, for providing safe, supportive transitional housing while empowering survivors of violence as they rebuild their lives.
Brave Noise beer is a COLLABORATION WITH A BIG GOAL: A SAFE AND DISCRIMINATION-FREE BEER INDUSTRY. Brave Noise is advocating for safe spaces and inclusive environments by requesting breweries be transparent with their policies and commit to long-term work. Our brewery code of conduct will also be easily accessible on our website and via the QR code located on the can label. Whiprsnapr will not tolerate or entertain sexism. bigotry, racism, or any other abuse and we hop you will join us in raising a pint to CHANGE and ACTION within the industry.
“All are welcome here”  https://whiprsnaprbrewingco.com/pages/our-values
 
 
Sara and Ian’s notes:
As parents of 3 girls, we are not blind to the fact that people who work in the hospitality industry – including breweries – are often treated terribly.  The stories being posted by Brienne Allen, and in Canada by Erin Broadfoot as well as others around the world, to us, are not surprising.  Horribly disappointing but not surprising.  All 3 of our girls work in hospitality and we hope and trust that they will recognize bad situations, raise alarm bells and get out as quickly as possible.  Being treated badly is not ok.  For anyone.  The hope with this Brave Noise collab release, is that enough attention is drawn to the industry to identify problems and make things better. 
For years now, you’ve supported us as we’ve collaborated on different beers and hosted a variety of fundraising events to help support women, BIPOC and LGBTQ communities and will continue to do so.  Thank you for your support, thank you for helping us be open and welcoming to all communities at whiprsnapr brewing co.
 
Oh the beer?  Sara and I brewed this on International Women’s Day and canned it this week. 
Brave Noise provided us with the recipe, but allowed for creative freedom…  so of course we exercised that option!  I wouldn’t really call this a pale ale, more of a NEIPA.  The recipe didn’t have any hops in the boil, but we like a little bitterness to help balance out all the big juicy hop flavours we get from the dry hopping so we added a little up front and some at the tail end of the boil.  The dry hop numbers were supposed to be equal parts sabro and mosaic at 2lbs/barrel, but as sabro has SUCH a strong presence, we did 2 parts mosaic to 1 part sabro.  Sabro can have a strong coconut profile, with fruity and citrus flavors while Mosaic has more of a blueberry/berry aroma, and notes of mango, stone fruit, and tropical bubblegum.
It is incredibly tasty, hope you enjoy.
