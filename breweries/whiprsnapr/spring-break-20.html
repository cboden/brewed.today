Unfiltrd Project Beer #107 - Spring Break 2.0 NEIPA 6% 45 IBU

Spring Break.  Imagine sitting poolside, sipping on a cool raspberry lemon cocktail.  Our Spring Break 2.0 NEIPA takes its inspiration from sunny days at your favourite vacation spot – even though you can’t be there… 

The nose is distinctly raspberry!  Your first sip will coat your tongue with a thick and juicy NEIPA, while the tangy raspberries dance on the tip of your tongue.  The flavour slides to the back of your tongue, and you'll get that tart lemon, having it border on a sour NEIPA! 

Truly unique, and super tasty, hope it helps take you away to a tropical destination (in your head).  Yup.  Back to back years of missing Spring Break….Groundhog day!

Keep refrigerated.  Drink FRESH!
