Keys Upon the Table. Rock’n’Hops Volume 8. 8% Double NEIPA.

Ian’s take:

Well you know we had to go big for 250… and is it 250? Probably not, but that’s what my poor counting has brought us too.

This one is THICK… and juicy… and just lovely. Sticky apricot, ripe berry, zesty citrus. Just so good.

Ok so what’s with the keys upon the table? And the Chinese food takeout box?!?!

Matt and I listen to a pile of music while brewing/canning/cleaning, and System of a Down has a song called ‘Chop Suey’ (ahem takeout box) which repeats ‘Why’d you leave the keys upon the table’ which is a bit of a running joke in our little world… total inside joke, but felt appropriate for #250.  IYKYK.
