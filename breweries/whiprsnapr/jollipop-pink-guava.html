Jollipop Pink Guava Milkshake IPA

6.2%  55 IBU

 

 

Another fan favourite, and second Jollipop release this year!  It’s been a few years since we made this one – and still had the classic labels kicking around!

 

Beer is supposed to be fun, and this is the ultimate in brewing up fun!  Big Milkshake IPA’s with unique and interesting fruits to deliver some super fun creations!

 

Pink Guava fruit is like bubblegum with tart aftertaste.

The body has loads of barley, oats and lactose, while Sterling hops bring the subtle bitter, the Citra hops deliver big tropical fruitiness. 

Super refreshing, cloudy, hazy, juicy... all that good stuff.

 

Time to have some fun.
