Chocolate Brownie Sundae.
Unfiltered Project #190
Chocolate Pastry Ice Cream Brown Stout… or whatever you wanna call it!
6% alc/vol 12 IBU
 

A chocolate lovers dream!  Thick, rich velvety mouthfeel, creamy chocolate with vanilla notes. 

Drinks like a big scoop of vanilla ice cream on a thick stack of double chocolate brownies!

CONTAINS LACTOSE.

 

Ian’s take:

Welcome to our first installment of the ‘Parlour Series’ where we take a tasty ice cream parlour treat and turn it into a beer!   It’s like being a kid all over again!  (for the brewer and the tasters!)

Chocolate Brownie Sundae.  Is it a stout…a brown… a pastry whatchamacallit… whatever it is, it legit tastes like a chocolate brownie sundae.

We mash with pure cocoa powder, English malts from Mississippi mills Malting with some dark chocolate barley and right off the hop (no pun intended – cuz this is not about the hops!) it’s a deep dark brown with the rich smell of cocoa in the air.  We added lactose and vanilla late in the boil to deliver that creamy sweet vanilla ice cream element to the beer, to help balance against all that chocolate.  We took it even further in the secondary by aging it on cocoa nibs, and added even more vanilla for good measure. 

It all adds up to a surprisingly balanced, not overly sweet – but beautifully rich beer that tastes exactly like a chocolate brownie sundae.
