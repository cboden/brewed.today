Unfiltrd Project #155 Perhaps, Perhaps, Perhaps IPA

6.5% alc./vol IBU 45

Another take on a music inspired offering. 

For those that don’t know Cake... you should get to know them.  Stylistically a bit out there, but very cool. 

This IPA is Vermont styled, a bit more bitter but packing a big candy and tropical hop profile.

 

Ian’s Notes:

Sneak attack!  Double release? 

Matt and I were canning one day, trying to come up with a name for this Vermont styled IPA, which is juicy, candy and tropical fruit flavoured – but not an opaque juice bomb.  There’s still a bit of bite, but just enough to keep it interesting.  Anyways… back to the name… our one off IPA’s and NEIPA’s have been typically more music oriented.  (ie: rocknhops, hop’em’all, back to school…etc) so were thinking along those lines.  Listening to music and canning – you have to be very selective about the music – because the canner is listening and reacts badly to the wrong bands.  Cake is one of those bands you can just listen to all day.  Just a super cool band full of sarcastic lyrics and deadpan vocals, and draw from wide-ranging musical influences, including country music, mariachi, rock, funk, Iranian folk music, and hip hop.  Their album ‘Fashion Nugget’ is one of my all-time favourite albums.  One song on the album is ‘Perhaps, Perhaps, Perhaps’.  Matt suggested this as the name for the beer, as apparently I say ‘Perhaps’ quite often when talking about ideas for new beers or whatever, then follow it up with the other 2 ‘Perhaps’ lol.  Listen to the song – you’ll get it.

And try the beer.  It’s amazing.

 

Available on tap, and cans to go.
