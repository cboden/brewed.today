Ottawa

Alt Schule

The Story...We’re going ‘Old School’ or Alt Schule for this Ottawa Oktoberfest special!Two years spent in Germany - we had our fair share of great beers - and great Altbiers.The Beer...For once - we went traditional.Our Altbier is a dark copper colour, full of German malts and German hops. Unlike most German beers, it uses a top-fermenting yeast called ‘Dusseldorf Alt’ which gives its flavour some fruitiness, but matured at a cooler temperature, which gives it a crisp and clean taste like a lager.
