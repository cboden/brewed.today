Ocean Beach West Coast IPA

6.2%  50 IBU

Ocean Beach.  San Diego, California.
Beautiful beach town with perfect weather every day.
Perfect place to enjoy an incredible West Coast IPA. 
Local Chinook hops with late/dry Amarillo hops.
 
Ian’s take.
We love west coast ipa’s.  I know everyone wants the juicy ones, but there’s nothing better than a great west coast ipa for its balance of clean drinking, bitter edge, hoppy bite and amazing aromatics.  West Coast IPA’s (moderate ABV/balanced ones) you can drink all day.  Hot day/cold day with pizza/steak anything and everything! 
Ocean Beach has a mix of old school/new school hops to put a twist on a classic west coast ipa.  Great piney / grapefruit citrus notes from the chinook hops, while the Amarillo adds a strong orange citrus element that really brightens it up.
 
The San Diego weather we were having last week may be gone, but this IPA will have you dreaming of the next hot spell!
 
The shot was taken on our trip to San Diego last year, on a gorgeous night strolling in Ocean Beach.
