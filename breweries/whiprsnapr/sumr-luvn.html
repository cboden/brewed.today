Sumr Luvn

The Story... Summer here is far too short. Make every minute of it, get outside - hit the beach, go camping or head to the cottage and enjoy all the amazing spaces we have around! The Beer... We wanted to maximize hop flavour in the lightest, lowest bitter levels you can get while still enjoying a super tasty beer. We used 2 row, oats, wheat and lactose - hopped with Citra and Amarillo hops. Flavour notes of peach, mandarin and passion fruit. Super sessionable - delicious and refreshing. Meant for summer afternoons.
