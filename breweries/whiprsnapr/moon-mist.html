Moon Mist Ice Cream Ale

Hey all you Bluenosers, we got something for ya.

Moon mist - your favourite ice cream, is now a beer!

Banana, bubblegum and grape flavours in a full flavoured dessert style ale.  Part of our Parlour series.

CONTAINS LACTOSE.

.

 
Brewer’s take.

This one scared the crap out of me.  I’d never had moon mist ice cream – still haven’t – but worked with a few east coast troublmakrs to come up with this very interesting flavour profile!

 

Banana.

Bubblegum.

Grape.

Ice Cream…

 

WTF.

 

In ice cream, you can have 3 distinct flavours slowly churned together to have a different flavour with each bite… beer… not so easy.

 

My initial thought was that you can get banana and bubblegum flavours from a hefenweizen styled beer – but you can also get clove depending on how it’s brewed, the barley to wheat ratio, amount of yeast and fermentation temperature!  So after heavy research and consulting with yeast provider, came up with a plan to execute.  Fingers firmly crossed as we went through the brew day, and fermentation… still wasn’t sure how I was going to introduce the grape though… throught about wine must, or using grape juice meant for wine… then thought, this ice cream is probably more focused on the candy-like grape flavours, less wine-like, so opted to use a pile of frozen minute maid grape punch! 

 

The result? 

Banana. Check!

Bubblegum. Check!

Grape. Check!

Ice Cream. Check!

Tasty?  You betcha!

 

It’s thick and creamy and loaded with layers of flavours, but I’ll leave it to the true Bluenosers to judge.
