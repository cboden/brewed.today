Whiprsnapr • Ottawa

GTFO 2024 8% West Coast IPA

$5.00 + tax

Bit of a tradition around here that started a long time ago. Used to be about getting rid of the in-laws, but turned into a kiss off to last year during the pandemic and seems to have stuck. 

Thought we were out of the woods, and not need to make another version post-pandemic - but here we are...

Ongoing war in Ukraine, the chaos around the Gaza strip and locally, the non-violent protests that always end in violence.

Then there’s Trump. Guess we’ll be making another of these in 2025.  Sigh.

There are better days ahead right!?!

Ian’s take:

Sequoia and Centennial hops featured here in this big West Coast IPA. Not as malty as in years past – and for me that’s a good thing.

Sequoia hops have some of the west coast features of citrus and pine that compliment Centennial’s candied pink grapefruit but also add a few tropical fruit notes.

Doesn’t come off boozy at all. Super smooth. Crisp and scarily easy drinking for an 8%!
