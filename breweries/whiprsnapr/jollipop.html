Jollipop Mango Milkshake IPA

6.2%  55 IBU

 

 

It’s all about the Mango baby!

A fruit that is the perfect balance of sweet and sour.

The body has loads of barley, oats and lactose.  Sterling hops bring the most subtle bitter, and the Citra hops deliver big tropical fruitiness. 

But cmon …this one is really all about the mango.

Super refreshing, cloudy, hazy, juicy... all that good stuff.

 

Ian’s take:

This really is crazy thick and fruit forward.  The highest ratio of fruit to beer we’ve ever done – it’s that thick.  That juicy.  That flavourful.

Perfect for the palletio or your home bbq.  Pairs great with spicy chicken.
