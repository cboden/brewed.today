Unfiltrd Project #153 Cliffs of Moher
Irish Cream Ale 5% alc./vol IBU 25
St.Patrick’s Day Irish Cream Ale.  Brewed using locally sourced 6-row malt for a true cream ale style.

Pouring brilliant copper with a foamy white head; the nose is malty and fresh while the palate is creamy with light raisin, plum and light bitterness. 

One of Ireland’s most famous sights

The Cliffs of Moher are entirely vertical and the cliff edge is abrupt. On a clear day, the views are tremendous, with the Aran Islands etched on the waters of Galway Bay. From the cliff edge, you can just hear the booming far below as the waves crash and gnaw at the soft shale and sandstone.
