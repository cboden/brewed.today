Sweet Hell

$4.42

Unfiltrd Project #110.  Sweet Hell.  IIPA 9% 91IBU. (Ian's bday beer!)

I love to ride.  I’m just not very good at it… especially the technical stuff.  Which is a bit of a shame, seeing as how I live a few hundred meters away from South March Highlands forest.  There are over 30km of rocks and roots in SMH, just waiting to put me on my ass. 

 

I am getting better-ish back there.  Not as good (or as stupid) as I was when I first moved here 15 years ago.  I’m too old for endo’s, so I mostly walk the features then crash and bang my way over the rest.  It beats the hell out of me – but I love it – and so does Joey.  He could go all day, every day.

 

In the winter time (with the fat bike) all that bump and grind gives way to silky smooth white ribbons of trail that I can ride all day.  Yeah – it’s cold, but there’s bugs in the summer, and rocks roots the rest of the year.  Winter fat biking is really the only thing I look forward to as the snow starts to fly.  I can’t wait to hit the trails in the winter.  Add another layer or two, and you’re good to go.  I’ve gone out in -25C temp with just 3 layers on.  Goggles on, pogies for the hands and feet wrapped in plastic bags – but it’s great fun on those super sunny winter days. 

 

This yin and yang, love / hate relationship I have with South March… is why I like to call it my ‘Sweet Hell’.

 

This weeks Unfiltrd Project is all about that ‘Sweet Hell’.  To best represent it, we decided to go with a BIG badass Imperial IPA.  9% and 91 IBU’s.  A blend of C-hops / Flaconer’s Flight in the boil, then dry hopped with Eukanot and Simcoe.  We kept the malt base super clean, to let the booze and hops shine.  A true West Coaster. 

 

Ian.

 

Special shout out to the Ottawa Mountain Bike Association (OMBA).  For years OMBA have been the caretakers of the South March Highlands forest trails.  Making some spots easier, others more ‘interesting’.  Lots of bridges, burms, skinnies and more recently trail markings – so you don’t zig, when you should have zagged.  Please look them up, give them a follow and sign up for a membership!
