Farmer In The Sky

Farmer in the Sky is a twist on a classic. We brewed a traditional German pilsner and dry-hopped it with Citra and the result is an amazingly clean and refreshing beer. Notes of citrus, lemon peel, and fresh cut grass are balanced beautifully with a subtle bit on the finish. As always: keep cold, drink fresh.
