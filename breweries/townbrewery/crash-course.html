Crash Course

$4.50

We decided to release three new beers for our 5th anniversary this year, one of which was an IPA. This likely isn't a huge surprise to anyone that has followed us for the last five years, as IPAs and working with hops has been a focus of ours since day one. We recently had the opportunity to get some new and highly touted hops out of New Zealand called Nectaron, which we immediately set aside for this brew. We paired these passionfruit and peach forward hops with our our most used, and favourite hop since we opened our doors, Mosaic. They combined to create a bright and tropical hazy that we believe is a great representation of the type of hop work we can do. Old meets new, you could say. A nod to the old friends who helped us to open our doors, establish ourselves and gives us the support we needed to get through those first few days, months and years; as well as the new friends who have continued to push us, helped us evolve, and reminded us who we are and what we set out to do. We appreciate you. ** Crash Course is defined as a rapid and intense course of study. We'd say this sums up the last 5+ years pretty well. We've learned and grown so much, but's it's scary and humbling to realize how much further we have to go.
