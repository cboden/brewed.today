Yeah Right! Our newest IPA is here, and we are 100% fired up about it. It's a nice big hazy beauty that we used to showcase two hops that we're really vibing on right now - Belma and Motueka. Belma is a hop variety from Washington State that has really beautiful aromas of tropical fruit, melon and berries. Motueka hails from New Zealand and is an explosion of big zesty limes. We paired these up with some OG studs - Citra and Centennial - which really helped to boost the citrusy punch and bring this whole thing together
