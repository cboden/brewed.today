First Rodeo

$4.65 + tax

A few weeks back we had the opportunity to get together with the fine people @rougeriverbrewing to collaborate on two different beers. We wanted to brew up a nice big hazy IPA for y'all but wanted to add in a little extra spice. We decided we would use a new hop product called Spectrum. Spectrum is a all liquid hop product that delivers true dry hop flavours and aroma. Imagine the finest hop pellets known to man, in liquid form. This is the first time we've ever used Spectrum - hence the name First Rodeo. Our half of the collab focused on Mosaic hops and wheat and it turned out great. It's a big juicy citrus bomb, with some background flavours of passionfruit and some subtle blackberry

6.3% alc./vol.
