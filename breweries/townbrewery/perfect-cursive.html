Perfect Cursive is an Italian Pilsner - brewed in collaboration with our good friends at Volo - inspired by the traditional brewing practices of the style using new age noble hop varieties. We started with a malt made from Bohemian spring barley creating a medium bodied, golden coloured base then layered in waves of Hallertau Tradition and Saphir hops. The result is a super flavourful beer with subtle notes of honey and spice, and a big bouquet of sweet floral aromatics jumping out of your glass. Combine that with a snappy bitterness and this one will have you coming back for more.
