Sunday Best

$5.50 + tax

It's been a minute since we put out a new Double IPA, and did we ever hit you with a banger here - introducing Sunday Best. We leaned on Simcoe Cryo to jam the whirlpool with more hops than we've ever put in a beer, then elevated that with a heavy dose of Phantasm powder to enhance the fruity thiols, and fermented it escarpment labs Thiol Libre to release those bad boys. The result is an intense hop bomb that's like a big 'ol slap to the face of all your six senses. The white wine character in this beer is unbelievable and it's followed up by enormous stone fruit and candied citrus. The body is big and the flavour is powerful. It's a potent lil' buddy and we think you'll love it. Enjoy!

8.2% alc./vol.
