Woodsy Wisdom

Woodsy Wisdom is a hazy yellow Pale Ale with a cloud-like fluffy white head. It starts with pronounced aromas of gushy nectarine, pine and lychee. The flavour is straight forward. Pulpy orange juice, tangerine peel and some of that sweet, sweet pine. The mouthfeel is creamy, pillowy and oh so soft. We're still trying to figure out how we can fill our pillowcases with it.
