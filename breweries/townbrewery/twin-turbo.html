TWIN TURBO

$5.25 + tax

Twin Turbo is a hazy Double IPA, brewed with our good friends at All My Friends Beer Co. in beautiful Bloomfield ON, as part of their 2026 Terptacular Collab releases. The idea was to create a whole bunch of big, dank and resinous beers using Terpenes from weed. 

Terpenes are the natural, aromatic chemical compounds found in cannabis that are responsible for the distinct scents and flavours of each strain. There's no THC or CBD here just big, potent aromas. We combined an all-star cast of hops - Nelson Sauvin, Rakau and Citra - with the terpene profile from Blackberry Kush and the results are kind of mind blowing. 

We're stoked to be a part of Terptacular this year alongside our good buds at All My Friends as well as Counterpart, Blood Brothers, Willibald, Third Moon and Le Ketch. Keep your eyes peeled across socials in the coming week as their versions are released.

8% alc./vol.
