One More Thing

$6.50

We've been thinking long and hard about how to expand our sour beer program and this is step one. It's a live sour using a new lactobacillus/yeast blend then fruited with blood orange and pomegranate (which we've been excited to use for a long time now). No hops used here, but the blood orange adds the perfect balance of sweetness and bitterness while the pomegranate adds a smooth fruitiness and beautiful colour.

It's the first beer we've put in a bottle in forever, and we plan to keep them coming. Grab a bottle, and enjoy the nice weather while it lasts!
