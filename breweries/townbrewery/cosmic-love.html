Cosmic Love

$5.50

Sour ale w/Raspberries and Passionfruit, dry hopped with Mosaic and Sabro

AROMA:  Hits you quick with the potent aromas of passionfruit and raspberry, followed by a quick hit of tropical citrus fruit, think tangerine and grapefruit - a result of the (heavier than usual for a sour) dry hop of Mosaic and Sabro

APPEARANCE:  Hazy pink with a pillowy white head...looks kind of like a popsicle and I am here for it

FLAVOUR:  Listen - I'm not gonna sit here and say this tastes like anything different than what it is.  Riding a heavy wave of passionfruit on a surfboard made of raspberries under bright lemony sunshine.  A smile in a glass!

MOUTHFEEL:  Big, bold and cheek puckering, slightly higher carbonation, very refreshing!
