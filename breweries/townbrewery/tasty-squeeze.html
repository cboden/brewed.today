Tasty Squeeze

$4.50

Back in December we sent an invite to our mailing list to see if anyone out there would be interested in spending the day with us brewing a beer. The feedback was unreal and our first Community Brew was born. We hopped on a video call to toss around some ideas (a five alive inspired IPA was the consensus winner) and all got together one Saturday to brew it up. Tasty Squeeze is a big juicy IPA fermented with a boat load of your favourite citrus juice. Traditional? No. Delicious? Hell yes. This beer is super refreshing and tasty and really makes you feel alive. Huge thank you to all of our amazing Community Brew participants - we had a blast doing this. Don't forget to sign up for our mailing list for your chance to be involved next time.We're also stoked to announce that proceeds from each can of tasty Squeeze sold will go to @GirlsInc
