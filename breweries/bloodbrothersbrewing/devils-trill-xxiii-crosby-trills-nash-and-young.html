DEVIL'S TRILL XXIII: CROSBY, TRILLS, NASH & YOUNG

$17.95

IIPA w/ El Dorado, Citiva & Pacifica • 8.5% ABV

Devil’s Trill has been a “long time gone,” but there is no need to feel “helpless.” I know you don’t want to “teach your children” from home any more and everyday feels like “deja vu” but we’ve got to “carry on.” Hop on the “Marrakesh Express” and head down to “our house” for an ice cold Devil’s Trill.This rendition features Citiva, a new hop on our roster. We paired Citiva with El Dorado and a touch of Pacifica for synergistic effects. Scents of ripe stone fruit are complimented with citrus, and tropical fruits on the palate. The body is full and smooth with restrained bitterness for drinkability.
