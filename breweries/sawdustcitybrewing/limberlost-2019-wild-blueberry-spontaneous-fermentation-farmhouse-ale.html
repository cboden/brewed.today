Sawdust City Brewing

Limberlost 2019

$14.11

Born in the wilds of the Limberlost forest this tart spontaneously fermented ale pours a beautiful deep blue and has aromas of fruit and dusty farmhouse funk. Tart without being too sour, it drinks very easily with the wild blueberry calming the beer down and balancing it out. The blueberry carries through to the finish and the lively carbonation gives it a nice dry finish. Limberlost is always one of my favourite releases of the year. There’s always a bit of anticipation mixed with anxiety on how it’s going to turn out. I mean, this beer is a total crap shoot, it’s fermented with yeast from the forest for cryin’ out loud! But we’ve been very fortunate to have it work out each year. And over the years we’ve definitely gotten better at blending and packaging this beer to make sure the final product comes out the way we intended. This years’ version is definitely our best one to date. It’s balanced, tart, fruit forward with a wonderful funk character. We’re hoping you love it as much as we do. Limberlost is always a fun experiment and it’s a wonderful way for us as brewers to learn and grow as professionals each year.
