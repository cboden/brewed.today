Metropolis Noir

$3.54 + tax

4.6% alc./vol.
Dark | Malty | Crispy

Pouring a deep, rich  brown, this winter lager has an off-tan head with gentle roasty aromas and a slight grassy hop character. Malty, without being heavy, the flavour continues with chewy malt notes and just a kiss of roast. Rich without  being cloying and crispy enough to enjoy after a game of pond hockey. Mellow and satisfying, it’s best accompanied with good friends by a fire or after a romp in the snow. Metropolis Noire winter lager is the perfect foil to the cold of the impending winter.
