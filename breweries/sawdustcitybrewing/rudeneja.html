Rudeneja

$31.66 + tax

7% alc./vol.
VIBRANT | BALANCED | MALTY

As summer fades, there’s a subtle shift in the world. It’s not just the outside we see changing, there’s also a calming internal transition. Rudejena is an old word meaning, “the world becoming Autumn”. We loved the idea so much, we captured it in a glass. 
A bold malt body pulls you in and warms your soul like a blanket. An audacious hop kick balances that warmth with vibrant flavours of berry, citrus and pine. 
Summer excitement is balanced by fall tranquility, like malt by hops. Drink the Rudejena.
