125 Gravenhurst Opera House - Amber Lager

$26.55 + tax

5% alc./vol.
SPICY | BALANCED | BREADY

Teaming up with the good folks at the Gravenhurst Opera House, we’re brewing a beer to celebrate the 125th Anniversary of Gravenhurst’s most iconic landmark.  This one is all about drinkability. A nicely balanced amber lager, 125 Lager has a malt-forward body with notes of toasted pretzels and roasted nuts with just enough spicy hops to keep the sweetness in check. When working with the Opera House, they asked for something that would be both approachable and great celebration beer. And we nailed it with this one. Perfect with whatever you’re celebrating or on its own, we think you’ll agree that this one is a winner.
