Do you love Pilsners?
'Cause if you do...

Join us starting at 7pm on Saturday, June 3, when we open 8 guest taps - of you guessed it, Pilsner. For the 2023 edition we are stepping up our party with live music from Toronto punk legends The Anti-Queens presented by Riverfest Elora.

What is Pilsfest?
Pilsfest is a celebration of our love for pilsners and it’s essentially a mini beer tasting festival. It's an advance-purchase, ticketed event.

A Pilsfest 2023 Ticket gets you:

One, 16oz Pilsfest glass to keep
Eight, 4oz sample tickets
Concert by The Anti-Queens
Choicest punk rock tunes by Dj Spenny
In-and-out privileges all night

Guest taps!!!

Burdock // Town Brewery // Tooth & Nail // Shortfinger // Brewer's Black Bird // Willibald // Still Fields // Sonnen Hill

How it works:

Join us anytime, starting at 7pm, on Saturday, June 3, come sample, mingle and enjoy good food and music
Just sign-in when you arrive (your name will be on the list) and we'll give you a glass and sample tickets
If you are buying multiple tickets, please include names of all attendees by adding them into "delivery instructions".
Have a good time and use hashtag #kill4pils to show your love for the Pilsfest
Keep your purchase email as back up (but we'll have your name on the list)
Must be 19+
***Tickets are NON-REFUNDABLE***
