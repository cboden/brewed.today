Highlander Brew CO.

Happy Landing

$3.25

Tasting notes: Happy Landing Diner & Gas Station was a staple in the South River and the Almaguin region for over 50 years. With the brewery now in this location, Happy Landing Ale pays homage to this landmark with an English golden ale orange rind and oatmeal to create a balanced well-crafted beer. Pairs well with: Light soups and salads, vegetarian dishes, Feta/goat cheese, sweet and fruity Asian dishes, citrus-flavored dishes, including dessert and salad dressings.
