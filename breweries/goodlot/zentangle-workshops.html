ZENTANGLE WORKSHOP

Hosted by Yvonne Westover from Ywestart

This workshop is being hosted twice. Please choose between:

Wed Feb 4

Wed Mar 25

Both sessions start at 6:30p and are approx 2 hrs in length.

Zentangle is a method of drawing patterns to create beautiful images. It is the process of simple lines, drawn repetitively, one stroke at a time, without a planned outcome, without expectations, quietly enjoying the pen marking the paper mindfully focussing on just that action there are no mistakes just opportunities.

Join us for a warm evening of creativity! Hosted in the Oast. The tickets cost includes the 2 hour session, a beverage of your choice and a takeaway bag of zentangle supplies so you can continue to practice at home.

$ 55 pp + tax.

About Your Instructor:

Yvonne Westover has been practicing art for over 40 years, has a degree in Visual Art, is a

Certified Zentangle Teacher and is an Instructor at Fleming College. She has taught in Singapore, Scotland

and the US. Yvonne knows that adding some creative time to your day will allow for a happy distraction that

can help reduce stress. Zentangle happens to be her go to practice to turn chaos into calm.

Yvonne's no fuss and supportive way of teaching allows for each participant to gain confidence in their own creative practice and skills.

Her work has been published and produced in books, magazines, calendars, stationery, and accessories.

Her work is in collections in Canada, US and Europe.

Yvonne is a collector of patterns & pebbles, the dessert menu is required reading and snow is her favourite season!
