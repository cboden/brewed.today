Sunshine City

$2.88 + tax

2021 Ontario Brewing Awards Bronze Medal for our flagship American Blonde Ale! It is bright and golden and comes in at 4.8%. It's friendly and sessionable, pairing perfectly with everything from grilled seafood to backyard campfires. That's why it is a MUST for all craft beer lovers!

Sunshine City Blonde is named after Canadian writer Stephen Leacock's literary references to the City of Orillia in his work "Sunshine Sketches of a Little Town" 1912. We honour the numerous and sometimes humorous usages of "Sunshine City" throughout the local community and, so too, embrace it as the namesake of one of our most popular brews.

First Released: Spring 2020

Suggested Pairings: burger and fries, cheese board, pizza
