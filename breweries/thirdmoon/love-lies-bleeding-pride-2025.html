Love Lies Bleeding (Pride 2025)

$23.01 + tax

"Love Lies Bleeding (Pride 2025)" IPA is our annual release brewed in collaboration with our friends @pflaghalton to celebrate Pride Month! The beer is a 6.5% Hazy IPA brewed with oats and wheat that was hopped with Ontario Cascade and Chinook from Backroads London as well as Sabro from @yakimachief.

PFLAG is a volunteer-run organization that serves the LGBTQ+ community and their loved ones and is committed to building safer spaces for queer and trans folks across Halton. We will be making a donation to PFLAG Halton for each can sold.
