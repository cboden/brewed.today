Our Bestowed Imperial stout series was created to bestow a rich bounty upon you.

"Café Del Bestowed" is a 13.5% Imperial Stout aged 14 months in a blend of Buffalo Trace and Weller bourbon barrels then conditioned on natural Ethiopian coffee.

This beer was created in collaboration with our friends from Nickel Brook in celebration of our 6th anniversary! It's our ode to their "Café Del Bastardo" which was an inspirational beer we enjoyed years before opening Third Moon. The coffee was roasted by our friends from Morning Roast Inc.

Serve at 14C for more aroma and flavour.
