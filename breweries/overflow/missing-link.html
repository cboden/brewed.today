Overflow • Ottawa

Missing Link

$3.32

Change is good, at least that’s what they say.  Our team is proud of the IPA’s we’ve brought to you and even awarded by some hardware over the years.  As per the style we’ve kept a balance of innovative flavours and aroma while still staying true to that ever so important ABV and IBU.  Our closest friends, family and staff have been asking for a beer that isn’t a light session IPA, nor that big ass Imperial Monster… We’ve been asked to bring a beer that is somewhere in the middle.  Lots of flavour and aroma, but also something that can be enjoyed by all.  Like that important transitional fossil that defined it all, we hope that we have found that “Missing Link” in our IPA line up.

Please take the time to enjoy this beer and as always, share your opinions with our team in the taproom, call us or email.  We love your input and as always, we aim to please!

ABV- 5.5%
IBU-55
This beer has aromas of apricot, grapefruit and fresh flowers.
The taste has hints of fresh grass, with a smooth biscuit like mid palate and a pleasantly bitter after taste reminiscent of grapefruit pith.
