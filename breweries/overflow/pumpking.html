PumpKING

What separates a leader from their kingdom...  It's what they are made of! The PumpKING exudes the real taste and aroma of that familiar festive dessert.  "The" KING is nothing short of the real deal.  We carved and pounded more than 50 KILO's of raw Pumpkin into this monster of a beer and hand ground fresh Ginger, Cinnamon, Clove, and Nutmeg!  We're super proud to be one of the few in the world to pull this type of seasonal beer off from locally sourced products.  We expect the King to rein for years as Leader of the Patch!

4.5% ABV
Pumpkin Kevik


Colour: Hazy Orange

Aroma: Is full of Pumpkin, Cinnamon, Clove and maintains hints of Orange, Nutmeg and Ginger...

Taste: Begins with something similar to a rich Pumpkin Pie filling and has a Graham Cracker and Autumn Herbal Tea finish.  You don’t need to try very hard with this beer to imagine that classic seasonal dessert.
