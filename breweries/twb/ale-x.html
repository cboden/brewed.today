Ale-X

$5.25 + tax

5.5% ABV - 473ml Can

Long before TWB had an actual brick & mortar location, when it was still a bit of hope and a dream, one of the founding worker owners, Alex, asked for a new beer for her birthday. Being a bit of a forest nut, she wanted a beer with trees in it - and that's how our Ale-X Juniper Rye ESB came to be!

To be fair, she expected a strong IPA with heavy pine notes - but the brewer took it in a totally different direction. That’s the lesson from this ale: sometimes a little collaborative creativity lands us something better than any one of us could come up with on our own!

A customer favourite from day one, Ale-X is a classic English-style ale. It's very approachable but unique, with a nice bite from the rye malt that balances the Juniper flavours. It’s a crisp, clear ale that pours a beautiful amber hue - perfect to share with a friend!
