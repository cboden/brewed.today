Brut to the Head

$7.00

Made in collaboration with our friend Adam Powell, this small batch made, bottle-conditioned Brut IPA features aromas and flavours of grapefruit, melon and white grapes backed by a cracker-like malt presence and finishes with a very dry and effervescent mouthfeel. Adam has been obsessed with creating a beer with this champagne-like character for some time and we think we nailed it pretty well here - this beer is fruity, sparkling, dry and not too bitter. Since the beer is bottle-conditioned, we encourage you to let it settle in the fridge for about a day before opening. When pouring, use care to keep the sediment at the bottom of the bottle for a sparkling appearance.

6.4% ABV / 20 IBU
