Dinko Fett - COMING SOON!

Do you say "Ahh-pricot" or do you say "A-pricot"? Either way, apricotcha back with this apricot milkshake IPA. Think of it as Mango Fett's "imaginary" friend - it's the same creamy, juicy base beer brewed with oats, lactose sugar and new-world hops but this time we amped it up with apricot puree and a few other hop varieties such as El Dorado and Mosaic. Expect a huge waft of fresh stone fruit aroma followed by a similar flavour profile that also includes tropical fruit flavours of mango and passion fruit to complement the star of the show: apricot! All of that is balanced perfectly against the creaminess of the lactose with a surprisingly smooth finish. Get it now before it becomes a figment of your imagination!
