People's Pint • Toronto

Pi is Exactly 3!

$7.00

This beer is part of our Third Anniversary Brew Series and was brewed by Angie, Chris, Deaglan and Meg also known as Bent Mallet Brewing.  In case you hadn't figured this out they are huge Futurama and Simpsons fans.  They present to you:

“Pi is Exactly 3!  For this special anniversary IPA, we wanted to bring you the best of both coasts - the juiciness of the East Coast with the bitterness and dryness of the West Coast. Add in the Sacch Trois yeast (and it had to be Trois, this is for the 3 year after all) which brings its own bright fruitiness, and all those qualities get amped up.

Now you can have your Pi and drink it too!”

6.7% ABV / 25 IBU
