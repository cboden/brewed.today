Homecoming

Homecoming is our tribute to Maris, our resident feline and just like her, this old-school APA has got a lot going on - it's hoppy, bitter, dry and refreshing. Toasty malt flavours meld with piney, grassy and citrusy hop flavours with a bit of spice and a tiny hint of caramel. A portion of the proceeds from this beer will be donated to the Ontario SPCA to celebrate the 1-year anniversary of Maris' "homecoming" to our family at People's Pint.
