People's Pint • Toronto

Forkuality

$32.00

The aroma of rich coffee is apparent on the pour with a faint note of toasted bread following. On the palate, this golden beer's complexity is immediately apparent with coffee dominating but giving way to a subtle citrus flair backed by toasted pumpernickel. A very easy to drink yet complex beer for a great cause - let it be your bright spot today!

#FORKUALITY is a movement that aims to raise awareness, support, monetary & material supplements of food, and access to food for everyone. Supporting food banks, temporary & emergency shelters, and remote communities. Working towards the end of food insecurity.

4.2% ABV  30 IBU
Blonde Stout w/ Coffee
