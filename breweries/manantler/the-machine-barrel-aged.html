The Machine Barrel Aged

Our Russian Imperial Stout, THE MACHINE, Aged one year in Cabernet Sauvignon Barrels from Stratus Winery in Niagara-on-the-Lake.

Big aromas of dried black cherry and dark roast coffee up front. The Cab. Sav. barrels illuminate the dark fruity characters of dark cherry, blackberry and cranberry, which are hidden in the original recipe. The big bold flavours consume the pallet yet allow each other to take the spotlight.
