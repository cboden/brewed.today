Toronto

Rosie

Mahogany in colour with hints of ruby shining through. An off-white head graces the liquid which gives it a majestic appearance much like an Australian Sheperd. Dark cherry leaps from the glass witch each swirl, which is followed up by raspberry and raisin with a hint of treacle. Moderately carbonated, yet soft in body, Rosie features flavours of candied fruits, more raisin and a slight phenolic note.
