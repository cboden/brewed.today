Style: Table Beer with Orange Peel and Loral Hops

Tiny Wooden Tables is the next in our Do Something Good Series.

It has notes of orange, spice, apricot and stone fruit.

We have decided to donate to the GRCA (Grand River Conservation Authority).
The Grand River Conservation Foundations goals are:
- empower the GRCA to achieve conservation results through recreation, education, and restoration,
- engage donors to support health and prosperity within the Grand River watershed, and
- develop a sustainable community for future generations
