River

$5.50 + tax

River is an IPA brewed in collaboration with Le Ketch brewery. They're located on the shores of the St Lawrence River in Sainte-Flavie, Quebec.

This past summer, while attending a beer festival in Montreal, we met Guillaume from Le Ketch and immediately felt a kinship in our breweries' philosophies, so we were happy to have him visit us to brew a beer. 

We macerated 10kg of PEC-grown wine skins and added them into the kettle and dry-hopped it with some mid-late harvest Nelson Sauvin and Cascade hops from New Zealand's Freestyle Hops, as well as a touch of Citra.

Our breweries are located nearly 1000 km apart on opposite sides of the St Lawrence, thus spawned the name “River”.

The beer is delicious! It's soft, too easy to drink and tastes like a white wine & fruit salad smoothie. Aromatics are popping out of this one.
