Limited Release Aged-Under-Ice Series
Ice - the solid state of the most powerful substance in existence on our planet. Liquid or solid, Water is an indomitable force of absolute awesomeness - Lake of the Woods Brewing Company uniquely puts both Ice and Water together with its innovative Deep Six Aged-Under-Ice Series.

Every fall prior to lake freeze-up, LOWBrewCo ’deep sixes’ hundreds of bottles of small batch, handcrafted beer to the bottom of Lake of the Woods. Placed below the ice line and secured in anchored cages, the beer receives the benefit of (at a minimum six month) bottle aging in near freezing - four degrees Celsius - watery lake current. This results in really cool beer.

2021 Beer Style: Russian Imperial Stout
Originally brewed for Catherine the Great, the Russian Imperial Stout is the one stout to rule them all. Born in darkness, bred in the north - Lake of the Woods Brewing Company’s Russian Imperial Stout was conditioned with toasted sugar maple wood and is riddled with rich and complex notes of dark chocolate, coffee, raisins, toffee and a hints of cherries and smoked maple. From LOWBrewCo with Love - best served with a thick accent.

INGREDIENTS: Water, barley, rye, hops, yeast. ABV: 10% | IBU: 75 | SRM: 40
