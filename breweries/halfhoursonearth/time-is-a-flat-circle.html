Seaforth

Time is a Flat Circle

This rustic light farmhouse beer underwent a primary fermentation with a Nordic Kveik strain known for its lager-like characteristics, and a secondary fermentation with Saison & Brettanomyces yeast over a 90 day fermenting/lagering schedule. Brewed with pilsner malt and flaked corn, hopped with Saaz and heavily dry-hopped with Hallertau Mittelfruh. The intention of this uniquely fermented beer is similar to that of our past farmhouse lagers, a super crisp, light and fluffy beverage for maximum refreshment. Trying to find the perfect balance of lager & Saison. Time is a Flat Circle hits all those targets but also adds a little extra fruitiness from the Kveik yeast. Notes of melon from the dry-hopping, a bit grassy, and the cold aging keeps the Brett funk to a minimum.
