Advanced Drinker - 5.0% Farmhouse Pilsner brewed with all organic ingredients & bottle conditioned with brettanomyces. Available only in 750 ml bottles. Advanced Drinker is a progression of our hybrid style of beers co-fermented with lager, saison & brettanomyces yeasts. This stainless fermented Farmhouse Pilsner was mashed in oak with 100% Organic German Pilsner Malt from Weyermann and hopped with all Organic BC hops from Harvesters of Organic Hops (@sam_hooh). We utilized Horizon & Ultra hops in the boil with a gentle Centennial dry-hopping. We're often asked what is our favourite beer we‚Äôve ever brewed, and we may finally have an answer to that. Advanced Drinker is first and foremost a Pilsner. Crisp, clean and refreshing with a toasted cracker malt character. A light malty sweetness up front, finished with a highly-attenuated dryness. The mouthfeel is soft throughout, yet the firm bitterness matches it perfectly. Notes of grainy pilsner, floral/spicy hops and faint citrus esters from the saison yeast. Our method for this type of beer continues to evolve. Initially cold fermented allowing our house German lager strain to create the profile for the beer. Nearing the end of fermentation we raise the temperature to allow the saison yeast to add a bit of Belgian influence while completing the fermentation. We then chill and lager for several weeks before dry-hopping and packaging with brettanomyces. Upon conditioning, the brett adds a very mild funk.
