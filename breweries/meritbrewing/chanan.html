MERIT • Hamilton

Chanan

$4.25

A special can release of Chanan for our Fourth Anniversary with 100% of proceeds being donated to Indwell, a Hamilton-based organization that supports unhoused populations by building affordable housing communities.  

Chanan is a beer that is made to honour my Grandfather, and the work that he and my Grandmother did in bringing over 1000 people from our village to Canada for a better life. They housed, clothed, fed, and employed everyone until they were able to go on their way securely and used their business, Merit Furniture, to facilitate this. I started brewing the beer that later became called Chanan as a beer that would pair perfectly with curries I would make at home. I wanted a beer that could stand up to, and add to, the depth of flavour and richness of many of my favourite curries so I started building that beer. Chanan is dry hopped – giving it bursting aromatics that match the intensity of aromas in a curry, it’s brewed with orange and Indian Coriander, two ingredients that compliment those used in some of my favourite dishes, and it’s fermented using a saison yeast that ensure a dry crisp finish that cleanses your pallet after each bite, resetting it to make the next bite feel fresh and vibrant again!

5.3% ABV - 355ml can
