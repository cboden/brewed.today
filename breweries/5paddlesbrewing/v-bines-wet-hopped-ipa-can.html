V Bines Wet Hopped IPA Can

$5.50

Wet hopping is a late September tradition for brewers.  Picking hops off the vine and putting them straight into the kettle.  Our West coast style IPA was brewed with a generous blend of wet Nugget, Cascade and Chinook hops which creates a fresh, grassy and fruity aroma.  Flavours are citrusy, pithy and piney.  This is as fresh as an IPA can be!
