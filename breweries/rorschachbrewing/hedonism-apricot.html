Hedonism - Apricot

Brewed with wheat, oats and lactose sugar. Hopped with Azacca and Eukanot. Kettle soured with lactobacillus and conditioned on hundreds of pounds of apricot puree with a hint of vanilla beans. Notes of apricot candy, stone fruit, citrus and a hint of pine. Full bodied and smooth with a juice-like balance of sweet and tangy. 6.9% ABV.
