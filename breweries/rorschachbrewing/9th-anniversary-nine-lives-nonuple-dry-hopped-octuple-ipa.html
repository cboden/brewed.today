Rorschach • Toronto

9th Anniversary: Nine Lives Nonuple Dry-hopped Octuple IPA

$6.86 + tax

We wanted to do something extra special for our ninth anniversary, so we figured we’d bring together nine of our friends, and some awesome IPA brewers, to create this excessively hop forward, tropical fruity Nonuple Dry-Hopped Octuple IPA. It has nine different hop varieties, selected by our friends, to push the limits of hop saturation. Here’s hoping we didn’t use up our ninth life with this one.

We figured we’d even add the lot numbers and hop varietals onto the can so you can have a sneak peak into our deliberations. Nine breweries, nine dry-hops and hopped at a rate of 11lbs/bbl equivalent with hops and extracted hop oils, this is by far the biggest, most hop saturated beer we’ve ever made!

Here’s a quick look at our friends who collaborated with us and their selected hops!

All My Friends: NZ Cascade Hop Kief
Badlands: Riwaka T90
Blood Brothers: Cascade HyperBoost
Counterpart: Peacharine T90
Fine Balance: Nelson Sauvin T90
Rouge River: Nelson Sauvin Cold Pressed Juice
Slake: Nelson Sauvin Hop Kief
Third Moon: Motueka T90
Willibald: Southern Cross T90

11.9% abv / 473mL
