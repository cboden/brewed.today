STRAWBERRY MILKSHAKE SOUR

$5.40

Strawberry Milkshake Sour.

Pours a deep garnet red with a quick light of white head. On the nose, strawberries galore, as if you were in a strawberry field surrounded by the ripe juicy fruit waiting to be picked. Taste follows the nose with loads of strawberry upfront, barbe rouge hops add to the ripe red fruit flavours. The lactic sourness provides a nice tartness that is perfectly suited to the sweetness of the strawberries; these flavours both linger into a relatively brief aftertaste. Medium bodied thanks to copious amounts of milk sugar, with zippy carbonation that provides a gentle fizziness to the brew's texture; relatively smooth, and effortless to toss back.
