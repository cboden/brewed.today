Broadhead • Orléans

PEACH WHEAT

$4.90

A smooth, approachable wheat ale made for easy drinking.

Pours a hazy, straw-golden hue with a soft, white head that sticks around just enough to keep things inviting.

Bursting with fresh peach aroma, supported by subtle tropical fruit notes and a touch of
vanilla. There’s a delicate wheatiness underneath that brings it all together without
overpowering.

The flavor opens with bright, ripe peach followed by soft wheat and just a touch of sweetness. The addition of vanilla and lactose adds a creamy, rounded finish that makes each sip smooth and satisfying. HBC 1019 brings vibrant stone fruit and light melon
notes, all backed by low bitterness for an easygoing, fruit-forward profile.

Light-medium body with a smooth, slightly creamy mouthfeel. Soft carbonation keeps it crisp without getting in the way. At 5% ABV, this beer is super crushable—especially as the weather warms up. Whether you’re soaking up sun on the patio or catching the first signs of spring, Peach Wheat is a refreshing go-to
