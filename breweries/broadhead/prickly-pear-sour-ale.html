PRICKLY PEAR SOUR ALE

$5.20

A beer made with Cactus?!
Heck yeah we did.
Don't be fooled by its thorny exterior; the prickly pear cactus boasts intensely sweet flavors of watermelon, strawberries, and raspberries.
Pouring a blazing strawberry pink, this sour is unfiltered to retain all the wonderful flavors of the fruit.
On the nose, you'll find a blend of watermelon, grapefruit, and raspberry, with a slight lactic sourness supporting in the background.
On the palate, those fruit flavors smack your tongue, along with a hint of passion fruit. The sip ends with a slight tartness to the finish, complementary more than sour.
This light-medium-bodied brew with moderate carbonation adds a delightful "prickly" mouthfeel to your drinking experience.
