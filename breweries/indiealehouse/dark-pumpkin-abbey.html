Abbey Ale - 8.5% 

Dark Pumpkin Abbey unites the classical elegance of a Belgian Dark Strong Ale with the festive warmth of Pumpkin Pie.
Undertones of chocolate, molasses and kola nut meld with yeast driven spice and orchard fruit, giving way to sweet, baked pumpkin and a hint of nutmeg, cinnamon, and clove. Soft carbonation and a slight residual sweetness lend a dessert-like creaminess to this decadent, seasonal treat.
