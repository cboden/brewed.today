Indie Alehouse Advent Calendar is back! - VERY LIMITED QUANTITY!

Each year the Beer Advent Calendar has been loads of fun to produce and share with customers, and great to hear from everyone on their favourite beers and discovering new beers with friends.
This year we have decided to produce only 48 Advent Calendars.

In the 2023 Indie Alehouse Beer Advent Calendar you will receive: 
- 24 DIFFERENT beers that have been individually wrapped and numbered, and a $25 gift card for use in 2024.
We are highly encouraging pick up to ensure safe and timely delivery, but understand this is not always possible, so NEW this year: Shipping is included but there is a free 6-pack of Tarana-Saurus IPA cans if you pick up your Advent Calendar at the brewpub! Pickup / shipping starts Nov 22, 2023. 

The approx. retail value of the 2023 Advent Calendar is $225 and is for sale for $175 + tax . We will sell out fast and we will not be able to make more, so get yours while they last!
This year the beers are not in numbered slots, so you will have to un-pack the boxes to find all of the beers. This will help us pack and ship without any issues, and then they can be stored cold in your fridge. We have tried to improve the product each year, taking in feedback from customers and the packing elves, so this year the calendar is a little different in packaging to help reduce waste, reduce wrapping damages, and still deliver you holiday cheer (aka. beer)!
