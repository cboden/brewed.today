Blackberry Smoked Wit
On our exploration of visiting breweries across Ontario, we instantly became friends with the fine folks over at Craft Heads Brewing Company in Windsor. 
Blown away by their work ethic, love for experimentation with beer, community engagement and outreach; we hit it off. When we get together it’s always a great time and good memories are created.
As a result of putting our heads together on what we wanted to brew, we are excited to present our Blackberry Smoked Wit.  An odd take on a traditional style. This wheat ale gives subtle notes of smoke and spice aromas. Then you’re hit with blackberry from the top rope. The finishing move on this beer will have you craving more sips of action. 
ABV: 5.3%Can Size: 473mL
