Indie Ale House • Toronto

Amaretto Glow

$10.00 + tax

Barrel-aged Cherry Amaretto Mulled Beer

This beer is a blend of Barrel-aged Mixed Fermented Ales that's been aged on Sweet Cherries and Almonds and was packaged still / un-carbonated. 

You can enjoy "as is", but we encourage you to add mulling spices like cinnamon, cloves, star anise, and orange peel, then served WARM. 

ABV: 7%Key Ingredients: Cherry, almondsBottle Size: 500mL

Preparation Instructions:

Step #1: Pour bottle contents into a pot or skillet on your stovetop.

Step #2: Add 1-2 teaspoon(s) of honey (or brown sugar), a pinch or a stick of cinnamon, clove, star anise (optional), and 1 orange peel. 

Step #3: Cook on your stovetop using a pot or skillet to warm gently (do not boil).

Step #4: Strain and serve!
