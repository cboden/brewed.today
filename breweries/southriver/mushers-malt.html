Mushers Malt

$3.50

Beer Type: English Brown Ale 

ABV: 5.4%
﻿IBU:  ﻿28

Tasting Notes:  A flavourful, malt-focused beer with hints of nuttiness, biscuit and caramel. One of our highest rated craft beers, Mushers Malt offers an overall gentle malt sweetness to create a well-balanced beer. This brew is an amber to reddish-brown colour with a moderate off-white to light tan head.
The Story: Mushers Malt pays tribute to the active sled dog community surrounding South River, Ontario. Each year, the Seguin Sled Dog Mail Run has takes place in February and has been doing so since 1985.  Envelopes or "Sledvelopes" are prepared and hauled by several dog teams to the Rosseau Post Office. People from all over come out to experience the old fashioned way of delivering mail in the North!

Best Food Pairings:  roasted pork, smoked sausage, grilled sausage, smoked or grilled fish, steak, nuts, Gouda cheese, and pears.

﻿Ingredients: ﻿Water, Malt, Yeast, Hops
